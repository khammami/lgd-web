## **Cookies**

Cookies are files with small amounts of data that may include an anonymous unique identifier. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device's internal memory.

Our Service does not explicitly use these "cookies." However, the app may use third-party code and libraries that use "cookies" to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to accept or refuse these cookies and know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If you choose to refuse our cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service. Please have a look at the third-party service's privacy policy for information on their use of cookies.
